FAQ
1.
How do I earn points and what are the rules?

1. Mandatory Task: Join the campaign on Galxe â†’ +100 points
2. Chatbot Task: Chat with two anonymous models, and cast your votes â†’ +10 points per vote
3. Referral Task: Invite friends with your referral link â†’ 50 points for both valid inviter & valid invitee.

Notes:
1. Joining the Galxe campaign is mandatory to earn Glacier Points.
2. You must use the SAME wallet address to complete tasks on both Glacier and Galxe.
3. For the Chatbot task, the maximum cap per address is 100 points per day, with a total limit of 500 points.
4. Please ensure your questions are meaningful, and remember to protect your privacy.
5. Valid Inviter: Users who have completed the Mandatory & Chatbot tasks and have valid invitees.
6. Valid Invitees: Users who log in to Glacier Network for the first time through a referral link and complete both the Mandatory & Chatbot tasks.

2.
What is Glacier Chatbot-Bench?

Glacier Chatbot-Bench is a benchmark platform designed to evaluate the performance of large language models (LLMs) on BNB Greenfield.

Participants can engage with two anonymous models, cast your vote on which provides the better response, and contribute to an unbiased rating systemâ€”all while earning rewards for your valuable feedback.

3.
How are airdrops distributed to participants?

The previous incentivized rewards will not disappear.

Participants will receive the GLS airdrops in proportion to their points after TGE. For more specific details, please refer to the Glacier Points System Guidance.

4.
What happens to the rewards we earned in previous campaigns?

The previous incentivized rewards are safe.

Additionally, for those who were part of the early builders program, you can continue to actively participate in the Points System to accumulate more rewards. The final airdrop will encompass the total rewards from all campaigns combined. Your previous incentivized rewards remain accessible here.
